What is Sustainable Living Hub?

The term "Sustainable Living Hub" refers to a concept or platform dedicated to promoting and facilitating sustainable and environmentally friendly lifestyles. It typically encompasses a variety of resources, information, and guidance aimed at individuals, communities, or businesses interested in reducing their environmental impact and adopting more sustainable practices.

Saving Our Earth in My Home

Recycle

Separate and recycle different types of waste to reduce landfill waste.

Compost

Start a compost bin for organic waste, which helps reduce methane emissions and enriches the soil.

Save Energy

Turn off lights and appliances when not in use, and switch to energy-efficient light bulbs and appliances.

Use Sustainable Transportation

Choose walking, biking, or public transportation over driving to reduce carbon emissions.
